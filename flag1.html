d. ZERO FLAG(Z): Zero Flag is the only0's present in the result of any Operation. If the result of Operation is 'only 0' then Zero Flag is Set to '1'.
If the result of Operation is 'Non-zero' then Zero Flag is Resetted to '0'.

e. SIGN FLAG:(S) Sign Flag is known as Replica of D7th bit.i.e. If the 'value of D7th bit in result is '0', then the Sign Flag will also be '0' and similarly
if the 'value of D7th bit is '1' then the Sign Flag is also '1'.
