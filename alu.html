Let's understand each element of Architecture in detail..


1) ALU(ARITHMETIC AND LOGICAL UNIT): ALU performs Arithmetic and Logical Operations on 8-bit of data. Following are the operations held by ALU:-
a. Addition
b. Subtraction
c. Logical AND
d. Logical OR
e. Logical EX-OR
f. Complement(Logical NOT) etc..

2) REGISTERS:
Registers are used for Temproary Storage and manipulation of data and instructions by the microprocessor.Data remain in the registers till they are sent to the I/O
devices or memory. Intel 8085 Microprocessor has the following registers:-
a. One 8-bit Accumulator(Reg A).
b. Six General Purpose Registers of 8-bit, these are B,C,D,E,H and L.
c. One 16-bit Stack Pointer(SP).
d. One 16-bit Program Counter(PC).
e. Instruction Register of 8-bit.
f. Temproary Register of 8-bit(including W and Z).
g. A set of the Flip-Flops(also known as status flags).
