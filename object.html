BASIC CONCEPTS OF OOP

1) OBJECT: Object is the collection of Run-time Entities present in the system. Objects and Classes take the memory space. Every Memory space has unique address assigned to it.

2) CLASSES: It is a collection of objects of similar types. e.g. Mango,Apple and Orange are the members of Class Fruit.

3) ENCAPSULATION: The wrapping up of data and functions into a single unit is known as Encapsulation.

4) DATA ABSTRACTION: Abstraction is the act of representing essential features without including the background details or explanation.

5) INHERITANCE: Inheritance is the process by which objects of one class acquires the properties of the objects of another class. It works on the property of 'Reusability'.
