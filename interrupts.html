Following two are the Serial I/O Ports.


21) Pin no.5(SID): SID stands for 'Serial Input Data'. It is used to accept serial data bit by bit from the External devices.

22) Pin no.4(SOD): SOD stands for 'Serial Output Data' . This is an O/P signal which enables the transmission of serial data bit by bit to the external devices.
